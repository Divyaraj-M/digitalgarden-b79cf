## What I'm Building — SparrowGenie

SparrowGenie helps B 2 B sales teams close deals faster using AI. We start where the pain is sharpest: **RFPs and proposals** — the high-stakes moments where speed, accuracy, and collaboration decide whether a deal moves forward or stalls.

Proposals are just the beginning. The long-term vision is to become the AI layer across the entire sales lifecycle.
